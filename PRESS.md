## Interviews

### Starry Mag — "Hans Christopher — Animal Kingdom" (June 26, 2022)
Interview about his six-episode arc as Auge in the final season of TNT's Animal Kingdom. Christopher discusses the character's arc, working within an established ensemble, and the physical demands of the role.
[Read on Starry Mag](https://starrymag.com/hans-christopher-animal-kingdom/)

### Occhimag — "Actor Hans Christopher Chats With Us About Waco and Beating the Odds" (September 11, 2018)
In-depth feature interview covering Christopher's breakthrough role as Randy Weaver in Paramount's Waco, his path from Sacramento to Los Angeles, and the realities of building an acting career.

### Blasting News — "Interview with Actor Hans Christopher" (June 24, 2018)
Early career interview exploring Christopher's transition from pre-med studies to acting, his craft philosophy, and his approach to preparing for dramatic roles.

### The AME Radio — "Hans Christopher" (September 5, 2018)
Radio interview covering Christopher's career trajectory, his upbringing in Sacramento, and his early work in short films before breaking through with Waco.
